Donation

New Life Church in Kampala, Uganda, is actively engaged in various initiatives to support the underprivileged in their community. If you're interested in contributing, here are some avenues you might consider: New Life Church Kampala New Life Church Kampala is dedicated to transforming lives through the message of Jesus Christ. They focus on vibrant worship, transformative teachings, and community support to empower individuals. Their ongoing campaigns include the Twezimbe Project, which aims to transform communities through holistic development, youth skilling programs, children's projects, and women's initiatives. These efforts are geared towards uplifting and empowering various segments of the community. NEWLIFECHURCHKAMPALA.ORG Twezimbe Africa Founded by Dr. Richmond Wandera, who also serves as the pastor of New Life Baptist Church in Kireka, Twezimbe Africa works to end generational poverty in Ugandan communities. Their mission encompasses teaching children, empowering women, equipping young adults, and improving communities, all while spreading the hope of the gospel. TWEZIMBE.ORG Pastor Raphael and Cathy Nguchie's Ministry Based in Kampala, Pastor Raphael and Cathy Nguchie have initiated ministries in northern Uganda's war-torn villages, leading to the planting of 33 churches. Their work addresses both spiritual and physical needs, including pastoral training, building classrooms for children, and acquiring land to support pastors' families. NEWLIFECHURCH.INFO

How to Donate

To support these initiatives: New Life Church Kampala: Visit their official website to learn more about their programs and donation options. NEWLIFECHURCHKAMPALA.ORG Twezimbe Africa: Information on how to get involved or donate can be found on their website. TWEZIMBE.ORG Pastor Raphael and Cathy Nguchie's Ministry: Donations can be made through New Life Church's giving platform, specifying support for the Uganda missions. NEWLIFECHURCH.INFO

Before making any contributions, it's advisable to contact these organizations directly to confirm the donation process and ensure your support reaches the intended programs.
