Sunday gatherings are a time of worship, fellowship, and spiritual renewal. Believers come together to praise God, hear His Word, and grow in faith. It’s a moment to connect, encourage one another, and build a strong community. Worship uplifts the soul, reminding us of God's goodness and faithfulness. The message strengthens hearts, providing wisdom and guidance for the week ahead. Prayer unites us, bringing peace, hope, and divine breakthroughs. Sunday is a sacred day to rest in God’s presence, seek His direction, and be refreshed for His purpose.

Looking for a church? New Life Church is a place of worship, community, and spiritual growth. It’s where lives are transformed through passionate worship, powerful biblical teachings, and genuine fellowship. Whether you're seeking a fresh start or a place to belong, New Life Church welcomes you with open arms. Come and experience God's presence, connect with others, and discover your purpose. Join us and be part of a loving family where faith grows, hope is restored, and lives are renewed.

Service to others is at the heart of New Life Church, reflecting Christ’s love through acts of kindness and compassion. We believe in serving our community, meeting needs, and sharing God’s grace in practical ways. Whether through outreach programs, volunteering, or simply being a light to those around us, we are called to make a difference. Serving strengthens faith, builds relationships, and brings joy as we follow Jesus’ example. Join us in spreading love, hope, and encouragement, knowing that every act of service glorifies God and transforms lives.
